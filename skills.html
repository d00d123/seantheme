Strategic Communications

During my time in communications at the Peel District School Board, I've been through crisis communications, labour communications, the launch of a new intranet site and the launch of a controversial update to the Ontario curriculum. User Experience Design

Web Content Creation

Sometimes the departments I work with ask for me to help create the content as well as organize it. I've had many opportunities to hone my skills at generating engaging web content. User Training

I provide intranet-management training to department webmasters within the board, to make sure they know how to manage their sites and to provide support when they run into problems. I also provide content and site management training to school staff who update their school's external website.
